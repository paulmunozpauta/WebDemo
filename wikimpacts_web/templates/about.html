About Wikimpacts

Wikimpacts is a tool developed by the Department of Water and Climate (HYDR) at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB) to explore and visualize the impacts of climate-related disasters around the world. Our mission is to provide accessible, data-driven insights to support decision-making in disaster risk reduction and climate adaptation strategies.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between raw data and actionable knowledge by providing a comprehensive platform that visualizes the global impacts of climate-related disasters. Our goal is to enhance awareness, preparedness, and response efforts among policymakers, researchers, and the public.

Our Team

Our team consists of experts in water and climate science, data analytics, and web development. We are dedicated to ensuring that the information provided by Wikimpacts is accurate, up-to-date, and relevant.
